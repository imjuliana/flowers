Our Journey

From a simple gift to a blossoming business

"I started in September 2025 making flowers for my special someone..."

The Beginning

It all started in September 2025 when I wanted to create something special for someone dear to me. I spent days learning, experimenting, and putting my heart into making the perfect handmade flowers. What began as a simple expression of affection turned out to be the seed of something beautiful.

The Turning Point

When friends and family saw what I made, they were amazed. "Why don't you try selling these?" they asked. "They're really beautiful!" At first, I hesitated. I was unsure if people would appreciate handmade flowers in a world of everything mass-produced.

But their encouragement stayed with me. After much thought, I decided to take that leap of faith. I created a few pieces and shared them online. To my surprise, people started ordering! That moment changed everything.

The Growth

From those first few orders, Julia's Craft began to grow. Each flower I make carries a piece of that original love and care. I've expanded from simple bouquets to various arrangements, each telling its own story.

Today, I continue to create with the same passion that started it all. Every order is special to me because I know it's not just about flowers‚Äîit's about connecting people, celebrating moments, and spreading beauty.
